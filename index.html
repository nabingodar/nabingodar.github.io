About Me

Hi, I'm Nabin Godar an ambivert with a curious mind and a passion for exploring both the world and the ideas that shape it. I enjoy meaningful conversations and connecting with people from different cultures, while also valuing quiet moments for reflection, learning, and personal growth. Originally from **Pokhara, Nepal**, I spent several years living in **Japan**, where I studied Information Technology and gained valuable academic, professional, and life experience. Living abroad broadened my perspective, strengthened my adaptability, and deepened my appreciation for different cultures and ways of thinking. Travel is one of my greatest passions. I enjoy discovering new destinations, experiencing local traditions, meeting people, and capturing memorable moments. Every journey teaches me something new and inspires me to see the world from a broader perspective. Beyond travel, I have a strong interest in artificial intelligence, science, psychology, philosophy, and the enduring questions surrounding consciousness, knowledge, and human existence. I believe that curiosity and lifelong learning are among the most valuable qualities we can cultivate. This website is my personal space to share my travel experiences, stories, photographs, and reflections. I hope my journey encourages others to explore new places, embrace different perspectives, and continue learning throughout life.
