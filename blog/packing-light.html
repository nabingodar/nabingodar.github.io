Packing Light — Tools for Travel and Thought

Aug 21, 2024 • Global

There is a discipline to carrying less. On a trip, each extra object taxes attention: where to store it, when to wash it, which pocket it lives in. Packing light is not only a physical economy; it's a mental one. The fewer objects you keep, the easier it is to notice new things.

I bring a small set of tools that travel well: a notebook, a compact camera, a multipurpose charger, and one book. Everything else is optional. Over time, I realized the most important item is not a gadget but a mood — curiosity without expectation.
